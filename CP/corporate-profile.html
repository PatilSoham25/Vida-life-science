Corporate Profile

Precision. Discipline.
Institutional Trust.

Vida Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd. is a healthcare-focused organization established in 2011, operating exclusively in medical sterilization and CSSD solutions.

Our Purpose in Healthcare

Healthcare environments demand precision and accountability. Vida Life Sciences supports infection prevention with structured sterilization systems.

  • Supporting patient safety
  • Enabling compliant CSSD operations
  • Delivering auditable systems

What We Do

Operating across the complete lifecycle of hospital sterilization and CSSD solutions.

CSSD Manufacturing

Manufacturing of medical devices, CSSD equipment, and SS 304/316L hospital furniture.

Turnkey Projects

End-to-end CSSD project planning, design, and implementation for hospitals.

Operational Support

Equipment maintenance, AMC/CMC services, and healthcare staff training.

Regulatory Commitment

Vida Life Sciences functions within a regulated medical framework and holds authorization to manufacture specified medical devices under the Indian Medical Devices Rules.

We operate with established quality management standards that support both national and international healthcare requirements. This compliance-driven approach ensures reliability and consistency.
